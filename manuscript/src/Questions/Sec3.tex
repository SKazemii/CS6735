As you can see, the algorithm had a great result on the cancer and mushroom dataset, while for other datasets, the results were not good. The reason could be either features are dependent or their distribution are not Gaussian.

\subsection{Iterative Dichotomiser 3 algorithm (ID3)}
The speed of building decision tree is relatively fast, the algorithm is simple, and the generated rules are easy to understand.
Disadvantages: In the selection of attributes, it tends to select those with multiple attribute values as split attributes, and these attributes are not necessarily the best split attributes; Cannot handle attributes with continuous values; Without pruning process, the decision tree cannot be optimized, and the generated decision tree may be over-fitting.
